Civic Issues & Policy


Political Advocacy: During the first half of the year, I worked with a team to create Penn State Paricipates, a group that aims to raise political awareness (factual knowledge) and increase engagement (inspire thought) through a unique and entertaining event to be known as PoliTinder. PoliTinder consisted of a life-size "maze" that directed you toward which political party that aligned with your ideals using a series a question. You can read more about it on The Collegian or WPSU, or check out our Facebook page!


Health Care Policy: In addition to my travel blog this semester, I have also been writing a Civic Issues Blog about Health Care Policy. Across four weeks, I wrote about Health Care Policy in the United States, Obamacare and Its Future Under Trump, Health Care Policy in Other Countries, and Health Care Ethics. You can check them out here.


Climate Change: Lastly, I have always been an adamant advocater for science. Under the current President's administration, climate change has been openly denied and I believe this to be a problem. For my Issue Brief and Advocacy Project for my CAS 138 class, I promoted the need for climate change awareness. I wrote a letter to Pennsylvania Senator Patrick Toomey about changing our current policy in climate change which you can read here. I also worked with my friend to design a children's iPad game to teach pre-school and elementary aged kids about climate change. You can see the concept slides here.
