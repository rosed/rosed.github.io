Hello!

My name is Rosy Zhang and I am student of the Schreyer Honors College at Pennsylvania State Universty, University Park in the College of Engineering Class of 2020. I am majoring in Computer Science (B.S.), Psychology (Neuroscience Option, B.S.), and minoring in Digital Media Trends and Analytics.

 

I am a Research Assitant in Dr. Kenneth Levy's Laboratory for Personality, Psychopathology, and Psychotherapy Research and Anxiety and Emotions Laboratory with Dr. Nicholas Jacobson in the Department of Psychology. I will also be an Orientation Leader for the International Student Orientation and Learning Assitant for Chemistry Principles II for Fall 2017.

 

Outside of academics, I also like to sing, dance, and play piano and violin in my free time. I am in the Campus Orchestra and K-Pop Music and Dance Club. I am also the Webmaster of Penn State's Global Brigades Envrionmental Chapter and a member of the Association of Women for Computing.
